About Me


Hi there! I'm Carson, a passionate, team-oriented game designer, programmer, and storyteller.

I have a strong drive for creating immersive and engaging gaming experiences, and actively want to push the boundaries of the medium.

I have honed my skills in a variety of programming languages and game engines, and am always seeking new opportunities to learn and grow. Whether I am developing a game from scratch or collaborating with a team of developers, I am constantly trying to test the possibilities of games.

I believe that video games have the power to bring people together, to challenge our perspectives, and to inspire us to be better. I am dedicated to using my skills and passion to create games that do just that.

Currently, I am attending the GIMM program at Boise State University and I can't wait to see where my journey in game development takes me next.
